FITNESS TIPS

Jo cheezein actually kaam karti hain — bina BS ke

01
PROTEIN KING HAI
Body weight ke per kg 1.6–2g protein lo. Muscle gain ke liye protein sabse zaroori hai. Dal, eggs, paneer, soya, chicken — ye sab achhe sources hain.
02
NEEND MAT CHODO
7-8 ghante neend mein muscles repair hoti hain. Kam neend = slow progress + cortisol badh jata hai = fat gain. Neend bhi workout hai bhai.
03
PAANI PIYO
3-4 litre paani daily. Dehydration se performance 20-30% drop hoti hai. Workout se pehle, during, aur baad mein paani zaroor piyo.
04
CONSISTENCY > INTENSITY
Ek mahine mein chamtkar nahi hota. Jo log 6-12 mahine consistent rehte hain, wahi actually transform hote hain. Choti choti progress celebrate karo.
05
PROGRESSIVE OVERLOAD
Har hafte thoda zyada weight ya reps add karo. Body ko challenge nahi milega to grow nahi karegi. Same weight same reps = zero progress.
06
JUNK FOOD CONTROL
80/20 rule — 80% clean khao, 20% enjoy karo. Complete restriction rebound karvata hai. Occasional cheat meal okay hai.
07
CARDIO + WEIGHTS DONO
Sirf cardio se muscle bhi jaati hai. Sirf weights se heart suffer karta hai. Dono ka balance raho best results ke liye.
08
STRESS MANAGE KARO
Cortisol (stress hormone) fat store karvata hai belly mein. Meditation, walking, ya koi bhi hobby — stress kam karo, results jaldi aayenge.
09
TRACK KARO PROGRESS
Weekly weight + measurements lo. Photos lo. Jo track hota hai woh improve hota hai. Scale pe nahi, mirror mein dekhna zaroori hai.
